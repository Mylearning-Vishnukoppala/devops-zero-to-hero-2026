## ❓ Behavioral Scenario Analysis

### Question: "Why does your current organization require DevOps methodologies?"
**Answer:** "In a legacy framework, engineering groups operate behind artificial barriers. Developers push feature versions rapidly without an operational understanding of deployment environments, resulting in the classic 'it worked fine on my local laptop' failure pattern. Operations teams are then left to resolve environment variations manually under production conditions. 

We introduce DevOps to transition from a reactive state to a proactive model. By implementing automated pipelines, standard testing matrices, and programmatic cloud guardrails, we ensure that delivery velocity scales linearly with application code quality, reducing time-to-market safely while lowering overall failure rates."
